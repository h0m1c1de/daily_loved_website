FAQ

What families usually want to know first.

Is DailyLoved medical care?⌄

No. DailyLoved is a non-medical companionship and check-in service. We do not provide medical treatment, diagnosis, medication administration, wound care, or clinical interventions.

What happens during a visit?⌄

A companion visits your loved one, spends time with them, observes how they seem to be doing, and sends you a follow-up update afterward.

Can you help with medications?⌄

We do not administer or handle medications. We can note what someone tells us about whether they took them, but all medication decisions and administration remain with the individual, family, or licensed provider.

What if something seems wrong during a visit?⌄

We will follow the contact and escalation instructions you provide during onboarding. In a suspected emergency, local emergency services should be contacted immediately.

What areas do you serve?⌄

We are launching on the Main Line in Pennsylvania, including Wayne, Villanova, Bryn Mawr, Berwyn, Malvern, Radnor, King of Prussia, Bridgeport, and nearby areas.

How quickly can someone start?⌄

Availability depends on your location and preferred schedule. Reach out and we will let you know what openings we have.
