FAQ

Clear boundaries, practical expectations, and what we do (and don’t) do.

Is DailyLoved medical care?⌄

No. DailyLoved is a non-medical companionship and check-in service. Companions do not provide medical treatment, diagnosis, medication administration, wound care, or clinical interventions.

Can you ask about medications?⌄

We will ask questions like “Did you take your meds as prescribed today?” and record the person’s response, without handling medications or making recommendations.

What if something seems wrong during a visit?⌄

You can define escalation rules (notify family, a neighbor, or an emergency contact). If an urgent emergency is suspected, the companion should follow your defined emergency protocol and local emergency guidance.

How do notifications work?⌄

The companion taps “Arrived” in the app, triggering an immediate alert. After the visit, the video and questionnaire are uploaded, and you receive another notification when they’re available.

Can you help with groceries?⌄

Yes, during the visit, the companion can help gather a grocery list and place an online order for delivery. Purchases and delivery fees are paid directly to the merchant/service.

What areas do you serve?⌄

We’re launching on the Main Line in Pennsylvania (Wayne, Villanova, Bryn Mawr, Berwyn, Malvern, King of Prussia, Bridgeport, and nearby). Join the waitlist with your ZIP code and we’ll confirm coverage as we open new zones.
